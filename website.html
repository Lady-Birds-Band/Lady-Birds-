About Us

We are Lady-Birds, a Riot Grrrl band ready to take over the world with raw, punk energy and feminist power! Formed in 2024, we bring grunge, rebellion, and DIY visuals. Our music challenges norms, smashes stereotypes, and empowers anyone who listens to it. From barbed wire lyrics to chains that bind us, we scream against the patriarchy!
